Platformer Quest

A precision-based platformer where timing is everything. Navigate through obstacles, avoid hazards, and restart instantly when you fail. Inspired by retro mechanics and modern web-based game development, this project explores the balance between frustration and mastery.

By Zanroe & Jeremiah


Shout out to ChatGPT for helping me figure out collision detection, player physics, and fixing a lot of bugs that came up while building the game. It really helped me do the troubleshooting and tricky interactions between platforms, enemies, and movement. It also helped me make the gameplay feel smoother and more consistent overall.
